Comprehensive Range of Services

Our emergency sewer line replacement in Eagle Pass includes a variety of sub-services tailored to meet all your needs:

Pipe Inspection and Diagnosis

Utilizing advanced camera technology, we inspect sewer lines to accurately diagnose issues without invasive digging, saving you time and money.

Trenchless Sewer Repair

Our trenchless techniques allow for efficient replacements with minimal disruption to your landscaping. This method is ideal for reducing the impact on your property's aesthetics and structure.

Pipe Relining and Bursting

  • Pipe Relining: We rehabilitate existing pipes with epoxy liners, creating a new, durable pipe within the old one.
  • Pipe Bursting: For severely damaged lines, we employ pipe bursting, simultaneously breaking the old pipe and installing a new one in its place.

Preventive Maintenance

To avert future emergencies, we offer routine maintenance services designed to keep your sewer lines in optimal condition. Regular checks and cleaning can prevent blockages and breakdowns, ensuring peace of mind.

The cost can vary greatly depending on the extent of the damage, the method of replacement, and location-specific factors, typically ranging from $3,000 to $10,000 or more.

The timeline for replacing a sewer line can take anywhere from one to three days, depending on the complexity of the project and any unforeseen complications.

The main methods include traditional trenching, trenchless pipe bursting, and pipe lining. Each method has its pros and cons, such as cost, landscape impact, and durability.

Typically, standard homeowners insurance does not cover sewer line replacement due to wear and tear. However, you may be covered if the damage is from a specific peril, or you might consider additional riders or sewer line insurance.
