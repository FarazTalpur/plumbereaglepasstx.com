Common Tap Issues We Resolve

Understanding the common issues that can occur with taps will help you identify when to reach out for emergency repair services. Here are some typical problems our team expertly handles:

  • Leaky Faucets: Often caused by worn-out washers, seals, or O-rings, leaking taps can waste significant amounts of water. Our technicians can quickly replace these components to stop the leak.

  • Low Water Pressure: Blocked aerators or supply lines can lead to reduced water flow. We assess and clean or replace affected components to restore normal water pressure.

  • Stiff Handles: Corrosion or mineral build-up can cause tap handles to become difficult to turn. We provide the necessary cleaning or replacement to ensure smooth operation.

  • Strange Noises: Whistling or squealing noises from your taps often indicate air trapped in the pipes or worn-out components. Our experts can diagnose and resolve these issues effectively.

Steps to Handle a Tap Emergency

Knowing what to do when you encounter a plumbing emergency can prevent further damage. Here’s a quick guide to managing tap issues before our team arrives:

  1. Shut Off Water Supply: Locate and turn off the water supply valve to prevent further leakage or wastage.

  2. Clear the Area: Remove any items around the affected tap to facilitate access for our repair team.

  3. Contain Leaks: Use a bucket or towels to catch any dripping water, minimizing potential water damage.

  4. Avoid DIY Fixes: While it’s tempting to fix things yourself, this can often lead to bigger problems. It’s best to wait for our professionals who are equipped with the right tools and knowledge.

  5. Call Us: Contact our team as soon as possible to ensure a rapid response. We’re here 24/7 to assist with your emergency needs.
